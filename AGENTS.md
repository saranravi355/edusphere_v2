# School Curriculum Context — PERMANENT

EduSphere 360 is being built for an **IB (International Baccalaureate) school**, not CBSE or IGCSE.
Always frame curriculum-specific content (grades, terminology, subject names, assessments) using IB
conventions: IB grade scale 1–7 (not percentages or letter grades) for DP, PYP/MYP phases or DP subject
groups (Group 1 Language A, Group 2 Language Acquisition, Group 3 Individuals & Societies, Group 4
Sciences, Group 5 Mathematics, Group 6 Arts), ATL (Approaches to Learning) skills, CAS (Creativity,
Activity, Service), EE (Extended Essay), TOK (Theory of Knowledge), Predicted Grades, IB Diploma Programme
(DP) / Middle Years Programme (MYP) / Primary Years Programme (PYP) terminology, and IB-style criterion
based assessment where relevant. Do not default to CBSE/IGCSE language (e.g. "percentage marks",
"board exam") unless explicitly asked to support a different curriculum later.
